DevOps in Intelygenz is a profile that singles out a team member with capabilities and skills, aimed at automation, definition and standardization and helps other developers achieve greater efficiency and quality in their work processes.

The DevOps provides other developers with the tools and processes that facilitate integration, continuous deployment and automated tests to help create environments, monitoring and, ultimately, entire ecosystems that help developers be responsible for their own code from their local environments to production environments.
